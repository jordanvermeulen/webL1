Surfing is a surface water sport, the wave rider or "surfer" rides on the front face of a wave carrying the surfer towards shore. The term "surfing" is the act of riding a wave and not the form in which the wave is ridden.

Hydrofoil is a lifting surface, or foil, that operates in water and is similar to aerofoils used by aeroplanes. Boats that use hydrofoil technology are also simply termed hydrofoils. As a hydrofoil craft gains speed, the hydrofoils lift the boat's hull out of the water, decreasing drag and allowing greater speeds.
